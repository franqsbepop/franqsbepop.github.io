Diogo Sousa Franquinho

Biography

Mathematics Ph.D. Candidate at Instituto Superior Técnico, Universidade de Lisboa (IST-UL). Doctoral scholarship holder from the FCT (Foundation for Science and Technology), research plan to be conducted at the Center for Computational and Stochastic Mathematics (CEMAT) and at the Korteweg-de Vries Institute for Mathematics (KdVI) under the supervision of Sven Karbach. MSc Stochastics and Financial Mathematics at University of Amsterdam and have experience in Quantitative Finance and Algorithmic Trading. Bachelors in Mathematics from Lancaster Univeristy under the supervision of Yemon Choi
